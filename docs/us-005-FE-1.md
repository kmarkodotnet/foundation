## Story

> As a **bejelentkezett felhasználó**, I want to configure which notifications I receive by email.

---

## Scope

Notification settings section on the profile page with toggle switches, saved via PUT API.

## API Consumed

| Method | URL | Body | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| `GET` | `/api/v1/auth/me` | — | `UserProfileDto` (tartalmaz prefs) |
| `PUT` | `/api/v1/auth/me/notification-preferences` | `NotificationPreferencesDto` | `NotificationPreferencesDto` |
