## Story

> As a **pályázati munkatárs**, I want to skip a skippable step with an optional reason so that the process can continue.

---

## Scope

Shared `SkipStepButtonComponent` reused across ContractGranter, VendorContracts, and Proof steps. Opens a modal for the skip reason; Admin/Elnök can restore the step.

---
