## Story

> As a **pályázati munkatárs**, I want to compose a budget plan with items so that spending is clearly defined.

---

## Scope

BudgetPlan step component with an item list, add/edit/delete item forms, drag-and-drop reordering, and a summary panel comparing planned vs awarded amount.
