## Story

> As an **elnök**, I want to approve the budget plan so that spending is confirmed before it starts.

---

## Scope

Reuses the shared `ApprovalPanelComponent` (from US-021) on the BudgetPlan step. Adds „Jóváhagyásra küldés" button for PalyazatiMunkatars.

---

## Implementation Checklist

  - `ApprovalPanelComponent` beágyazása (Elnök/Admin számára látható)
