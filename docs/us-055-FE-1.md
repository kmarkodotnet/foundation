## Story

> As a **pályázati munkatárs**, I want to record a vendor contract so that the service engagement is documented.

## Scope

VendorContracts step component with a list of contracts, add contract form (with vendor autocomplete), optional BudgetItem link, and total summary panel. Skip step via shared SkipStepButtonComponent.
