## Story

> As an **adminisztrátor**, I want to deactivate a vendor so that inactive companies don't clutter selection lists.

---

## Scope

Deactivate/reactivate button on Vendor detail page. Warning if active contracts exist. Mirrors the Granter deactivation pattern (US-102-FE-1).

---
