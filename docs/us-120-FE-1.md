## Story

> As an **adminisztrátor**, I want to manage codelist items (add, edit, deactivate, reorder).

---

## Scope

Codelists admin page. Codelist selector on the left, item management on the right with CDK drag-and-drop reordering.

---

## API Consumed

| Method | URL | Body | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| `GET` | `/api/v1/code-lists` | — | `CodeListDto[]` |
| `GET` | `/api/v1/code-lists/:id/items` | — | `CodeListItemDto[]` |
| `POST` | `/api/v1/code-lists/:id/items` | `CreateCodeListItemRequest` | `CodeListItemDto` |
| `PUT` | `/api/v1/code-lists/:id/items/:itemId` | `UpdateCodeListItemRequest` | `CodeListItemDto` |
| `PATCH` | `/api/v1/code-lists/:id/items/:itemId/deactivate` | — | 204 |
| `PATCH` | `/api/v1/code-lists/:id/items/:itemId/activate` | — | 204 |
| `PUT` | `/api/v1/code-lists/:id/items/reorder` | `{ orderedItemIds }` | 200 |
